Computer

A laptop is a portable computer designed for convenience, combining performance and mobility in one compact device. It typically includes a built-in screen, keyboard, and touchpad, making it easy to use without external accessories. Laptops are powered by rechargeable batteries, allowing users to work or enjoy entertainment on the go. They come in various sizes and specifications, catering to students, professionals, and gamers alike. Modern laptops often feature powerful processors, ample storage, and advanced graphics, enabling multitasking and creative work. Their versatility makes them essential tools for education, business, and personal use in today’s fast-paced digital world.
