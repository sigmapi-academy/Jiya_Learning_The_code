Coffee

Coffee is a beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide for its rich flavor and energizing qualities. Made from roasted coffee beans, it can be brewed in various styles such as espresso, cappuccino, or cold brew, each offering a unique taste experience. Beyond its stimulating caffeine content, coffee is often associated with comfort, conversation, and productivity. Many cultures have developed traditions around coffee, from Italian cafés to Ethiopian ceremonies. It also contains antioxidants that may benefit health when consumed in moderation. Whether sipped slowly in the morning or shared socially, coffee remains a universal symbol of warmth, focus, and connection.
